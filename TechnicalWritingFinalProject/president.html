How to Play President

President is a card game that you can play with your friends. It is a simple game based on both luck and skill. You will absolutely love playing it.



Objective

The objective of this game is to play all your cards. The first person to have no cards left is the winner.



What you need:

Items Quantity
Standard 52-card Deck (jokers are optional) 1
Players Minimum of 4







Game Concepts

   -  Order of cards from lowest to highest is 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King, Ace, 2, and joker

   -  Singles, doubles, triples, and quadruples

        -  Singles, doubles, triples, and quadruplets are the number of cards played at a time

        -  Singles means one card at a time

        -  Doubles means two cards at a time

        -  Triples means three cards at a time

        -  Quadruples mean four cards at a time

        -  The first person of the round will play one of the four options and for that round it will continue. For example, if            the first person plays four cards, everyone else for that round will have to play four cards

        -  If multiple cards are being played, they all have to be the same number or value. For example, if you are            playing triples, it can’t just be any three cards; it has to be the same number or value such as, three fours, or            three kings. An exception to his rule is for 2’s and jokers. A single joker can be played on any number of cards.            A single 2 can be played on a single or double. Two 2’s are required for a triple, and three 2’s are required for            quadruples.



  -  Your last card played from your hand cannot be a 2 or you automatically lose

  -  First place is president, second place is vice president, last place is scum, second last is vice scum, and everyone      else is neutral

  -  Before the next game starts, but the cards have been dealt, you need to trade some cards.

        -  The scum gives the president their two highest cards

        -  The president gives the scum two cards that they don’t want

        -  The vice scum gives the president their highest card

        -  The vice president gives the scum a card that they don’t want

        -  Neutrals will not have to trade cards



Game Structure

  -  The person with the three of spades starts first

        -  They don’t need to start with the three of spades; it can be any card or cards, but they go first


  -  One person will play a card, and someone else will play a card that is higher than the card just played. For      example, if the first person played a 3, the second person can play any card from 4 and up, but not a 3.

  -  Whether you go clockwise or counter-clockwise does not matter for the first game.
