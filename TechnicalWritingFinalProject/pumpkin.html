How To Carve A Pumpkin



Drawing the Image


  1. Draw an outline of an image of your choosing on the pumpkin with a permanent marker. Most people use an image of a scary face (See Figure 1). You can use a stencil or freehand the drawing.


    2. Removing the Guts


    1. Cut out a circle at the top of the pumpkin around the stem with a knife or a tool of your choosing, and remove the top. Keep the top. Do NOT throw it out.
    2. Scoop out the insides with a spoon until inside is nice and smooth. You can keep the seeds and roast them if you’d like.


    Cutting Out the Image


    1. Take the tool you used to cut the top of the pumpkin out and carve out your image by tracing the outline that you made earlier at, “Drawing the Image.”
    2. Remove the parts that you cut out.

    You can also add a candle to light up the pumpkin by putting a candle inside the pumpkin at the bottom standing up, so it doesn’t fall.
