About Me

My name is Erika May, and I was born in Toronto. I moved to Winnipeg five years ago. It’s different here, but it’s still Canada, so there wasn’t much of a culture shock. My mom is Japanese, which makes me half. Her entire side of the family lives in Japan, so we used to go every year, but because of COVID, we haven’t been in two years.

I decided to enroll in the software development program because I really enjoy coding, and creating games and websites. I also didn’t enjoy networking very much when I did it last year and the year before. The hardware and the technical stuff about a computer isn’t something I would choose to learn about, and I wasn’t a fan of it at all.


I am very passionate about music. I love listening to songs, and I like to play the piano and sing when no one’s around. I have a subscription to Apple Music, not Spotify, and every week Apple Music shows me how many hours I’ve listened to so far this year. As of this past Sunday, I’ve listened to 536 hours of music, and last year, I listened to a total of 953 hours. I was so close to 1000.
